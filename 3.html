My Friend George


Read in the newspaper a man killed by porphyria，

It reminds me of my friend George.


People say this man is five feet six inches tall,

It sounds like George and his porphyria face.


I knew George since he's 7,

I always thought that he was great.


Anything that George would do

I would do it too.


George liked food and George liked to fight,

He eats at the front every night.


I'd eat with him when work has done,

We are overweight but it was all in fun.


Colonists, he'll kill your friends and family

to remind you of his love.


You have to surrender to My Friend George,

Surrender to My Friend George!
